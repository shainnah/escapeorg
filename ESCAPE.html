GOALS

ESCAPE, short for (E)xtended (S)ervices and (C)are (Ap)proach for (E)veryone, was created mainly for reaching out those people who are incapable of consulting medical professionals due to factors including financial problems and lack of accessibility or knowledge about the importance of seeking health care. Being a non-profit organization, it targets to deliver the right and quality assistance for those residents living in community area around Quezon Province and even those who are living on the streets, being exposed to various risks of diseases. More so, it will be one of the sources to help people "escape" the struggling healthcare system of the country.

Resources Expansion

As part of maximizing the limited medical supplies and services, this organization desires to find strategies in giving contribution upon donations, fund raising activities, and even opening for potential sponsors that can help increase the healthcare resources. Thereafter, it seeks to implement programs such as free check-ups and supplies that can benefit the health of community people.

Consistent Programs

As mentioned, programs will be planned and facilitated to improve the activities done by the organization. In relation, it aims to be consistent throughout the process to ensure that beneficiaries will acquire the medical assistance in timely and quality manner.

End Social Stigmas

With the existence of discrimination in the access of healthcare, ESCAPE will lead in making sure that everyone will be taken care of by appropriate and respective medical professionals.

Fair Treatment

One of the values practiced in this group is equity. Withal, no one will be deprived of the services and care extension.

CURRENT PLAN

FUN-ding

Various Recreational Activities

Lists of activities that are considered include Basketball Leagues in different barangays, Zumba for a Cause, Literary Competitions, Variety Show, and Music Fest.

Care2Care

Taking Care of Medical Professionals

Free medical check-ups and assistance will be available for healthworkers.

Reach Out

Annual Community Home Visit

To provide sincerity and quality service, healthworkers and/or volunteers will do personal visitations in community people for at least 4-5 times a month.

H.I.M.

Heal In Mind

Apart from health education, spiritual and mental healing will be given priority through focus-group encounters. This activity will be done in respect of every religious beliefs of the people. And to be able to adhere with this consideration, everyone will be heard and careful topics will be aligned.

About US

Shainnah B. Villanueva | Founder of ESCAPE

A second-year nursing student from Sacred Heart College who aspire to expand the health services and offer free basic check-ups for people in the community, living in the streets, or those who stay in areas far from the medical facilities in the municipalities around Quezon Province. With the background about struggling healthcare system, she is encouraging volunteers not just to improve the activities of ESCAPE, but to inspire people especially the youth to give the right to healthcare to everyone regardless of age, living class, income, etc. As of now, ESCAPE has 25 members and counting who sincerely participate to accomplish the goal of this organization.
